Dynamic Physiology: 3 Core Processes

💧 Filtration

Hydrostatic pressure drives fluid into Bowman's space. GFR ~125 mL/min. Glomerular capillary endothelium, basement membrane, podocyte slits form barrier.

Watch: Glomerular Filtration Dynamics

🔄 Reabsorption

Active transport reclaims water, glucose, ions with 99% efficiency. Na+/K+ ATPase drives secondary active transport in PCT.

Watch: Tubular Reabsorption Mechanism

🧪 Secretion

Adds H⁺, K⁺, drugs into tubule; essential for acid-base homeostasis. Occurs mainly in proximal and distal tubules.

Watch: Tubular Secretion & pH Regulation
